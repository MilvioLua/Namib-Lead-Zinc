BOARD OF DIRECTORS & EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT


Asser
Kapere
Non-Executive Chairman

Asser Kapere joined the Board in mid-2016. He was appointed as the High Commissioner to the Republic of Botswana in late 2018....Mr Kapere has in the past served in several senior political positions and offices in Namibia following independence, including in the Cabinet as Deputy Minister for Works, Transport and Communication from 2002 to 2004 and then Chairman of the National Council of Namibia from December 2004 to 2015. He was the Chairman and Founder of the Erongo Development Foundation, a non-profit organisation involved in poverty alleviation efforts in the Erongo Region.

James
Beams
Non-Executive Director

James Beams joined North River having previously spent 14 years with Anglo American plc. For his final five years at Anglo American he was the ...Chief Financial Officer for the Copper division, and prior to that served in several senior management positions within the company. Prior to his time at Anglo American, he held senior finance roles at Minorco and Commercial Union. James is current the Chief Executive Officer of Piran Resources Limited.

Ratonda
Katjivikua
Non-Executive Director

Ratonda Katjivikua joined the Board in mid-2016. She previously held the position of Human Relations Manager and then Corporate Relations ...Manager for Langer Heinrich Uranium (Pty) Ltd, a uranium mining company in Namibia which is a member of the Paladin Energy Ltd group from October 2006 to December 2015. She had wide ranging involvement in the management team, including strategic planning, human resources and organisation development, stakeholder relations, and corporate social development programmes.

Dag
Kullmann
Managing Director

Dag Kullmann is a mining engineer with 30 years' experience in the mining industry, predominantly in Southern Africa. Before joining North River ...Resources in 2017, he spent 10 years in Namibia with the Forsys Metals Group of Companies, initially as project and mining manager, then also its general manager. Prior to this, Dag spent 18 years in South Africa, first working in the Wits gold fields and finally 8 years consulting with Snowden Mining Industry Consultants on a wide variety of commodities across Africa and further abroad.

Kudakwashe
Hartzenberg
Acting Chief
Financial Officer

Kudakwashe Hartzenberg has 16 years of experience in financial services, oil & gas, mining and FCMG in which she held positions from ...management accountant to senior management roles where she was responsible for the entire finance function and has also provided consulting services to the Ministry of National Planning Commission. Kudakwashe holds a B. Accounting (University of Namibia), MBA (University of Stellenbosch Business School), Advanced Diploma Management Accounting (CIMA UK). She has completed the International Executive Development Program (IEDP) with the University of Witwatersrand Business School in partnership with the London Business School and Bangalore Business School in India.

Elvid
Kaveru
Mining
Manager

Sarel
Malan
Plant
Manager

Sarel Malan is a metallurgical engineer with 15 years’ experience in the Namibian mining industry and an additional 3 years in the ...South African mining industry. Before joining Namib Lead & Zinc in 2018, he spent 8 years in uranium, working for Paladin, Rio Tinto and CGNPC in various managerial positions, partaking in the design, construction and commissioning of three processing plants. Prior to this, Sarel spent 7 years in base metals and diamonds where his responsibilities ranged from optimization, production to technical and always in managerial capacity. He also had tenures in other metals production like chrome, gold, platinum and manganese. Sarel holds a B.Sc. Hons. In Metallurgical Engineering which he obtained at the University of Pretoria.

Sydney
Garoëb
Mineral
Resource Manager

Sydney has over 14 years in mineral extractive industry, mainly in hard rock environment (both in open pit and underground) for various ...commodities such as gold, uranium and base metals.
